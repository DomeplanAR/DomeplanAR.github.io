What is DomeplanAR?

DomeplanAR is an automated BIM (Building Information Modelling) configuration tool for geodesic structure planning. It goes beyond traditional 3D modelling & design, allowing full control of the geodome’s 3D model, down to the uttermost details - nuts & bolts, with fully automated design features.

Create your geodesic structure model in under 10 minutes

Time is always of the essence! Designing a 3D model takes time and, in the case of geodesic structures, can become a redundant and repetitive task for planners. DomeplanAR automates the whole design process, allowing you to create a 3D model of geodesic structures with simple parametric inputs, in a fraction of the time it would take to manually design one.

BIM me up Scotty

Classical 3D modelling doesn’t take into account all the small details that make up the real, end-product. In the case of constructions, not having enough data on the project at hand leads to unforeseen construction costs, with final projects ending up to cost as much as 200% of the initial estimation. BIM mitigates all those unforeseen costs, by using the more accurate data to provide better cost estimates. BIM modelling also works to mitigate risks related to structural, MEP (plumbing, pipes and electrical) and safety concerns. It allows a full and complete overview of the construction, in all its details.

Design without designing

Ah, the beauty of automation! Simply put, you transform 1000 clicks into 1. Pro or amateur, anyone can now generate a 3D model for a geodome. The future of 3D modelling is now - generative design will forever change the way we model and build, bringing the knowledge of Gods down to Earth.

Ready to build

Not just the model, but also the material list - you get everything that you need to start constructing your geodome on spot. Besides being able to generate the 3D model of the geodesic dome to spec, DomeplanAR will also instantly generate the precise material list - number & types of struts, connectors, base area and surface of covering material - ready to build!

A world of Geodomes

Glamping

Glamping is a nowadays rising trend in terms of tourism. The concept of combining a luxurious habitation module in the heart of nature, with spectacular scenery, opens the way for a new paradigm in touristic experience.

In the world of glamping, the geodome is king. With several options to choose from when creating a glamping site, the geodesic dome comes out as the “go-to” option for glamping modules, given its unique properties - being easy to assemble, light & resistant and offering the largest amount of space vs. materials used.

Your backyard greenhouse

More and more people are reorienting themselves toward home-grown vegetables & fruits, focusing on sustainable living. The need for an efficient permaculture greenhouse at home drives people in search of options that can both efficient in crop management and sustainable in the long term.

Already home-growers are starting to adopt the geodome as a sustainable alternative to other structure designs for greenhouses. Unlike classical greenhouse design, the geodome is much more resistant and cover more surface, with less building material, giving more room to maneuver and for crop growth. Moreover, it’s easy to assemble and to fit in any space.
